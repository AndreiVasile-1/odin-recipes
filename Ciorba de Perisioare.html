Ciorba de Perisioare

The classic meatball soup consists of a clear broth, often with

pieces of or whole meatballs with vegetables; common

additions are pasta (e.g., noodles, although almost any form can

be used), dumplings, or grains such as rice and barley. Various

types of meat are used, such as beef, lamb, pork and poultry.

  • 1 courgette
